    • DanZ-AIO: Varus Release
      • Core: Playstyle System
        • Select your build's playstyle to activate unique logic tailored for each mode: ADC / On-Hit, Lethality (Poke), and AP Burst.
        • Prioritize Target with Most Blight: An intelligent targeting option that automatically focuses the enemy with the most Blight stacks, ensuring your detonations are always used on the most vulnerable target.
        • Max DPS Sequence: A toggle for each playstyle to enable a devastating, pre-programmed R -> W -> Q -> E combo. When active, it executes a frame-perfect sequence to one-shot key targets the moment your ultimate is available.
      • Q (Piercing Arrow):
        • Features a unique, custom-built prediction system developed from the ground up. It analyzes target movement, velocity, and pathing to ensure Piercing Arrow lands with maximum accuracy, even on erratic targets.
        • Killsteal: Smart logic that calculates Q damage (including Blight detonation) to automatically secure kills that would otherwise escape.
        • Poke Keys: Dedicated hotkeys for different situations: a standard key for mana-efficient Q poke, and a separate key for a high-damage, empowered W+Q poke.
        • AP Options:
          • Hold Q Release: Maximizes AP burst by holding the arrow until the target accumulates 3 Blight stacks for a devastating detonation.
          • CC Safety Release: A crucial safety feature that instantly releases a charging Q if you get hit by crowd control, preventing you from being animation-locked and vulnerable.
        • Farming: Highly configurable sliders to control when to use Q for clearing minion waves or jungle camps, based on the number of minions it will hit.
      • W (Blighted Quiver):
        • Auto-Activate: Seamlessly activates W the moment you begin charging Q, ensuring your arrow is always empowered for maximum damage without needing an extra key press.
        • Execute Only Logic: A mana-saving feature, perfect for the AP playstyle, that only allows W activation if the subsequent empowered Q is calculated to be a killing blow.
        • ADC Combo Detonation: Allows the ADC to perfectly weave in an empowered W->Q detonation once a target has enough Blight stacks from auto-attacks, controlled by a minimum stack slider.
      • E (Hail of Arrows):
        • Utility:
          • Auto E on Immobile: Instantly blankets rooted or stunned targets with a slowing field, extending the duration of the lockdown.
          • Anti-Gapclose: Stops assassins and divers in their tracks by intelligently placing E directly in their path to halt their advance.
        • ADC High-Speed Logic:
          • Combo Opener: Option to start fights with E to apply an immediate slow and begin stacking Blight.
          • High-AS DPS Protection: Automatically disables E at high attack speeds to prevent the cast animation from interrupting your auto-attacks, ensuring maximum DPS.
        • Detonation: Provides an instant, reliable way to detonate Blight stacks—especially useful when Q is on cooldown or when the AOE slow and healing reduction are needed.
      • R (Chain of Corruption):
        • Combo Logic: Fully configurable R usage to match your strategy:
          • Always Engage: To start a fight on the first opportunity.
          • Only if Target is Killable: As a finishing move when the full combo is fatal.
          • If it Hits X Enemies: For landing game-changing, multi-person ultimates in teamfights.
        • Auto-Usage:
          • Auto R on immobile targets for guaranteed lockdown.
          • Use R to interrupt important enemy channels like Katarina's ultimate.
          • Use R defensively to stop a champion from diving you.
        • Semi-Manual Cast: A dedicated hotkey that gives you full control for clutch plays, firing R at the highest-priority champion near your mouse with a single key press.
      • Visuals & Drawings:
        • Status Display (HUD): A clean, on-screen Heads-Up Display to see your current script settings (Playstyle, R Logic, etc.) at a glance, without opening the menu.
        • Spell Ranges: Clear, vibrant indicators for Q, E, and R ranges so you always know your effective threat radius.
        • Damage Indicator: A live calculation on the enemy health bar showing if your full combo is lethal, helping you make the crucial decision to engage or disengage.
        • Blight Stacks: Draws a visual counter above enemies, helping you know the perfect moment to detonate for maximum damage.
